What am I seeing?

  • Tone is the emotional/cognitive state inferred from context.
  • Strategy tells the renderer how to sound: drone, particles or pulse.
  • Intensity controls energy, texture and modulation.
  • Confidence is how strongly the agent recognized the pattern.
